Who We Are

Health Charity Foundation was founded to address the growing health and social challenges faced by the people of South Sudan. In a nation plagued by conflict and economic hardship, HCF empowers vulnerable communities, women in particular, children and the elderly through programs that promote health, education, and sustainable livelihoods.

Our Vision

A world where communities are transformed and resilient, barriers to health and well-being are removed, and everyone has equal opportunities to prosper.

Our Mission

To work with vulnerable communities to discover opportunities that improve their health and well-being, help them recover from crises, and support them in creating solutions to address vulnerabilities and inequalities.
