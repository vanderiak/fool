Empowering Communities. Restoring Health. Building Resilience.

Health Charity Foundation is an non-profit organisation based in South Sudan, dedicated to improving the health and well-being of vulnerable communities.We work with families, women, and youth to create sustainable solutions in health, education, food security, and sanitation.Through dialogue, empowerment, and community-driven action, HCF strives to build resilient communities where everyone has the opportunity to live a healthy, dignified, and productive life.

Our Initiatives

Empowering and Building Resilient Communities

We focus on three core areas to create lasting change:

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene

We enhance access to clean water and hygiene.

Food Security & Nutrition

We combat hunger and malnutrition.

Primary Health Care & Adolescent Health

We aim to build a resilient health system that focuses on women, children, and adolescents.
